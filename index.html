Who We Are

Waguma Life Outreach UG is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable communities in Uganda. Our mission is to provide essential services such as healthcare, education, and economic empowerment to those in need.

We have been working tirelessly to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty and inequality. Our team is composed of passionate individuals who are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of the people we serve.

We believe in the power of community and collaboration, and we work closely with local partners to ensure that our programs are culturally sensitive and effective. Together, we can create a brighter future for Uganda's communities.

Mpigi Medical Camp: Screening & Support

On November 2, 2025, Waguma Life Outreach UG and Elsa Africa hosted a medical camp in Nakilebe (Kololo-Kyetabya), Mpigi. Community members received free screening for TB, sickle cell, malaria, dental, and cancer. Our team also distributed essential items including clothes, school fees, shelter, soap, sanitary towels, sugar, and Maam Kits. The event fostered hope and improved health for many families.
